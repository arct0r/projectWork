    217 mg/mÂ³
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The DNEL for systemic effects via the inhaled route is determined on the basis of route-to route extrapolation from the oral combined repeated dose toxicity study with the reproduction / developmental toxicity screening test, conducted according to OECD Test Guideline 422 and in compliance with GLP, with dimethylsilanediol (DMSD).

    The following correction was made to the NOAEL (oral): Correction respiratory volume rat (24 hour) 1/1.15 m3/kg bw/day. Therefore the corrected NOAEC for repeated-dose systemic effects via the inhalation route is: 250 mg/kg/day*(1/1.15) = 217 mg/m3.

    AF for differences in duration of exposure
    1
    Justification

    The NOAEC for the nasal irritation was 15 ppm (54.6 mg/m3) in a 28-day inhalation toxicity study in rats exposed 6 hours per day (Naas, 1996). This NOAEC is in the range of the NOAEC of 9-27 ppm derived from the chronic inhalation toxicity study in rats exposed 24 hours per day for 2 years (Heicklen et al., 1981) indicating that the nasal irritation is a concentration- but not exposure duration-related effect. Therefore no assessment factor will be applied to take into account the daily or total exposure duration.

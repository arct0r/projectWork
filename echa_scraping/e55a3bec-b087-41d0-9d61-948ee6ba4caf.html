    Additional information - General Population

    As per E.C.H.A.Guidance on information requirements and chemical safety assessment Chapter R.8: Characterisation of the dose [concentration]-response for human health Section R 8.1.2.5. Duration of exposure, page 16, Acute exposure General Population DNELs are not necessary. Furthermore, General Population DNELs are not required because the Chemical Safety Assessment demonstrated that there is little or no General Population exposure to the substance,2,3 -epoxypropyl o-tolyl ether via any relevant route of exposure. In addition, quantitative dermal exposure study data is lacking. .

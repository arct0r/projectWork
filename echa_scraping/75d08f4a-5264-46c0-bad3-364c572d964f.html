    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The oral NOAEL was converted, using ECHA Guidance (specifically: Example B.3 of Appendix 8-2 of Chapter R.8., Version 2.1; November 2012), to the corrected inhalation NOAEC. The inputs were as follows: sRV rat = 0.38 m3/kg-day (for 8-hours); ABS oral-rat = 50%; ABS inhalation-human = 100%; and sRVhuman/wRV = 0.67. This gave the corrected inhalation NOAEC = 5.3 mg/m3.

    200

    NOAEL
    Value
    700 mg/kg bw/day
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    772 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The dose descriptor starting point (NOAEL) was obtained from a study performed with Neodecanoic acid. This value was recalculated under consideration of the iron content in Neodecanoic acid, iron salt, which resulted in modified dose descriptor starting point of 772 mg/kg bw/day.

    AF for dose response relationship
    1
    Justification

    Appropriate NOAEL

    AF for differences in duration of exposure
    2
    Justification

    Extreapolation from sub-chronic to chronic duration necessary, since study was performed for 13 weeks

    AF for interspecies differences (allometric scaling)
    4
    Justification

    Allometric scaling from rat to humans

    AF for other interspecies differences
    2.5
    Justification

    Remaining differences

    AF for intraspecies differences
    10
    Justification

    Assessment factor accounts for differences in general population

    AF for the quality of the whole database
    1
    Justification

    Robust 90-day study performed under GLP and according to the relevant OECD guideline 408

    AF for remaining uncertainties
    1
    Justification

    No remaining uncertainties assumed

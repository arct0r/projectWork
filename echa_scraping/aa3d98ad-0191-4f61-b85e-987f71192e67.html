    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Modification of the starting point.

    Key study selected: OECD 407 study performed with undecylenoyl glycine => NOAEL for systemic toxicity = 150 mg/kg bw/d.

    corrected inhalatory NOAEC = 150 x 1/0.38 x 6.7/10 = 264.47 mg/m3

    (assuming 100 % absorption for both routes in both species)

    Correction for inhalation to oral absorption: no factor is added. Justification: the test substance has a low volatility (vapour pressure of less than 0.5 kPa). - No difference in oral and inhalation absorption is expected between rats and human

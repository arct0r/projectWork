    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    2 -Methoxyethyl 2-cyanoacrylate is a reactive monomer that polymerizes in presence of moisture under usual working conditions. Polymerization can only be avoided by keeping the compound in a moisture-free, closed system. Due to quick polymerisation on contact with moisture, only negligible bioavailability of the monomer is expected. Testing of local effects on skin and eye demonstrated no hazard. Considering the widespread industrial and domestic use of glues containing cyanoacrylates no specific hazard is expected for 2 -methoxyethyl 2 -cyanoacrylate.

    Additional information - General Population

    2 -Methoxyethyl 2 -cyanoacrylate is a reactive monomer that polymerizes in presence of moisture under usual working conditions. Polymerization can only be avoided by keeping the compound in a moisture-free, closed system. Quick polymerization results in very low bioavailability, hence, no systemic toxicity is expected. Investigation of local effects demonstrated no hazard.

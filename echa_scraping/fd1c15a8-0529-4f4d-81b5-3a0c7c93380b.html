    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - General Population

    The test substance is manufactured and used under strictly controlled conditions over the entire lifecycle and no exposure via inhalation, dermal or oral route to the general population is expected. Therefore, no DNELs for the general population are required.

    Furthermore, the test item is not incorporated in an article and the manufacturer can demonstrate and document for all relevant scenarios that throughout the life cycle strictly controlled as well as rigorously contained conditions as set out in Article 18(4)(a) to (f) (Regulation 1907/2006) apply.

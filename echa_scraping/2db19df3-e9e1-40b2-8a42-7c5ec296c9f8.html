    Additional information - General Population

    The substance is used as a co-formulant in plant protection products. These products are only available for professional use, and direct exposure of the general population to the substance is very unlikely. Members of the general public may be only indirectly exposed as bystanders or residents as a result of the professional use of plant protection products containing the substance.

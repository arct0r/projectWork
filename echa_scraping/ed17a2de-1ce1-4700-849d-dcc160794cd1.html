    Additional information - workers

    No data are available which would allow derivation of DNELs for short-term or long-term exposure. No generally accepted methodology exist to derive a DNEL from acute (single dose) toxicity data (see ECHA Guidance on Information Requirements and Chemical Safety Assessment). No repeated dose study is available for the substance (not required for isolated transported intermediates).

    Additional information - workers

    In order to evaluate toxicological properties of the substance Octanoic acid, zinc salt, basic, information on its two moieties, zinc cation and octanoate anion, liberated upon dissolution, were considered. For a documentation and justification of that approach, please refer to the information given in the assessment entities and to the separate document attached to section 13, namely Read Across Assessment Report for Octanoic acid, zinc salt, basic.

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The following corrections are made to the LOAEL (oral) to derive the LOAEC (inhalation) for dimethoxy(methyl)vinylsilane:  

    Respiratory volume rat = 0.38 m³/kg bw/8 hr day. Respiratory volume human = 6.7 m³/day. Respiratory volume worker = 10 m³/day. Bioavailability in the rat and human is assumed equal. Oral absorption in the rat and inhalation absorption in the human are assumed equal. Therefore, the corrected LOAEC for repeat-dose systemic effects via the inhalation route is: 50 mg/kg bw/day x (1/0.38) x (6.7/10) = 88 mg/m³.

    Additional information - workers

    The worker DNELs for long-term systemic effects via the inhalation and dermal routes are derived from the combined repeated dose toxicity study with reproduction and developmental toxicity screening test, conducted according to OECD Test Guideline 422 and in compliance with GLP, with oral gavage administration of the registered substance dimethoxy(methyl)vinylsilane. In this study, a systemic NOAEL of lower than 50 mg/kg bw/day was established.

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The following corrections are made to the LOAEL (oral) to derive the LOAEC (inhalation) for the test substance dimethoxy(methyl)vinylsilane:  

     

    Respiratory volume rat = 1.15 m³/kg bw/24 hr day. Bioavailability in the rat and human is assumed equal. Oral absorption in the rat and inhalation absorption in the human are assumed equal. Therefore, the corrected general population LOAEC for repeat-dose systemic effects via the inhalation route is: 50 mg/kg bw/day x (1/1.15) = 44 mg/m³.  

     

    For infrequent DNELs for the inhalation route, the corrected general population LOAEC for repeat-dose systemic effects via the inhalation route is also: 50 mg/kg bw/day x (1/1.15) = 44 mg/m³. However, the assessment factor ‘AF for differences in duration of exposure’ is reduced from 6 to 1, and the overall assessment factor reduced to 75. Therefore, the infrequent DNEL for the inhalation route is = 0.58 mg/m³.

    A NOAEL of 2500 ppm (equivalent to 175.7 mg/kg/day) was determined for the substance tin chloride (CAS no. 7772-99-8) in a sub-chronic repeated-dose toxicity study (oral) in rats (OECD TG 408, GLP).

    Additional information - workers

    DNEL derivation was performed in accordance to the Guidance on information requirements and chemical safety assessment Chapter R.8 Characterisation of dose [concentration]-response for human health.

    For workers no acute toxicity DNEL or DMEL for long term exposure - local effects needs to be derived as no specific acute toxicity hazard is identified and sodium hypophosphite does not cause irritation, corrosion and/or sensitization, respectively. Also no information on dose-response relationship is available.

     

    No worker-DNEL long-term for dermal route-systemic and inhalation route were derived as no specific long term toxicity hazard has been identified.

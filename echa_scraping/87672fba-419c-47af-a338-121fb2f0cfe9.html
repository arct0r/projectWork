    Additional information - General Population

    Reason for data waving on general population:

    This substances use is limited as monomer substance for thermoplastics polymer, especially ABS resins, and the residual monomer concentration should be less than 0.1 % in polymer. So, for the consumer, the unreacted residual monomer in a polymer is to be regarded as impurities that need not be critically addressed in the exposure assessment.

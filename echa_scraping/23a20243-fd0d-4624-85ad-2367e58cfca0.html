    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The no-observed-adverse-effect-concentration (NOAEC) for long-term systemic inhalative exposure was calculated based on a no-observed-adverse-effect-level (NOAEL) of 105 mg/kg bw/day determined in a subchronic (90-day) oral repeated dose toxicity study. Route-to-route extrapolation accounted for corrections for differences in respiratory volume (2.63), differences in bioavailability (0.5), light acivity at work (0.67) and differences between human and experimental exposure (1.4).

    NOAEC = 105 x 2.63 x 0.5 x 0.67 x 1.4 = 130 mg/m3

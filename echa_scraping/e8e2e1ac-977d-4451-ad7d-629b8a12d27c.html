    Additional information - workers

    A DNEL was not derived - instead the AGW (ELW) of the TRGS 430 was used.

    This is 0.018 mg/m NCO

    For 3,4-Dichlorphenyl isocyanat (MG = 188.01 g/mol) this corresponds with 0.080 mg/m 3,4-Dichlorphenyl isocyanat

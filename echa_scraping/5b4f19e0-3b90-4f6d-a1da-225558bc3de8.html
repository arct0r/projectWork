    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    medium hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    1,3,2-Dioxathiolane, 2,2-dioxide is suspected of causing cancer based on the available data, including carcinogenic literature data on the substance and structurally similar substances, QSAR predictions (including structural alerts). Its has also been identified as a possible alkylating agent.

    Due to the evidence of carcinogenic potential the substance should be used under rigorous risk management measures to minimize exposure to workers.

    The available data was considered insufficient to derive DMEL so a qualitative assessment will be performed.

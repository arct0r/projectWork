    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    low hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    As the submitted substance is classified as irritating to the eyes (Eye Irr. 2, H319 according to the Regulation (EC) 1272/2008) with no adverse systemic effects observed after a single administration or a repeated exposure (even at the limit dose of 1000 mg/kg bw in the repeated dose toxicity study), only a qualitative risk assessment is required for the worker.

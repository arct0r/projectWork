    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    high hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    For long term effects the most critical endpoint of mutagenicity was taken. No quantitative dose descriptor is available for gentic defects. As such no DMEL can be derived and no quantitative risk assessment is possible. All acute effects are based on the potential for sensitization and eye corrosion.

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The NOAEC of inhalation is derived from the NOAEL of development toxicity / teratogenicity test. According to REACH R.8 Figure R. 8-3, corrected inhalatory NOAEC = oral NOAEL * (1/sRV rat) * (ABS oral-rat/ABS inh-rat) * (ABS inh-rat/ABS inh-human) *(sRV human/wRV) * (Days per week(experimental)/Days per week(worker)) = 1000 / 0.38 * 0.5 * 0.67 *1.4= 1230 mg/m3. Days per week(experimental)/Days per week(worker) = 7 / 5 = 1.4.

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The NOAEL of dermal is derived from the NOAEL of development toxicity / teratogenicity test. According to REACH R.8, corrected dermal NOAEL = oral NOAEL * (ABS oral-rat/ABS dermal-rat) *(ABS dermal-rat/ABS dermal-human) * (Days per week(experimental)/Days per week(worker)) = 1000 * 1 *1 *1.4 = 1400 mg/kg bw/day. ABS oral-rat/ ABS dermal-rat = 1; ABS dermal-rat/ ABS dermal-human = 1; Days per week(experimental)/Days per week(worker) = 7/5 = 1.4.

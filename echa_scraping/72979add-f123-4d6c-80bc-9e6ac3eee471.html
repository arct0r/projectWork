    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    [Empty]
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The submission substance showed some signs of acute toxicity in respective short-term tests, but no dose-response information is available, which would allow to derive DNELs. A qualitative risk characterisation is performed in the Chemical Safety Report for these endpoints.

    Information from repeated dose toxicity studies is not available, as these studies are not required, based on provisions according to REACH Annex XI, 3.2 (Substance-tailored, exposure-driven testing). Therefore no basis for deriving DNELs for long-term human health effects is available.

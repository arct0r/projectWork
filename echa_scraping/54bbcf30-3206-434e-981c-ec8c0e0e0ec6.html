    General Population - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - General Population

    No adverse effects were observed in the oral 28day repeated dose / reproduction / developmental toxicity study in rats up to the highest dose tested.

    Furthermore, the substance is neither classified for acute oral toxicity and acute dermal toxicity nor for eye irritation and sensitiziation.

    The substance has been classified as skin irritant Cat. 2 based on an in vitro test and therefore is given a low hazard rating for local effects.

    A qualitative risk assessment is considered sufficient for the general population's exposure to the substance.

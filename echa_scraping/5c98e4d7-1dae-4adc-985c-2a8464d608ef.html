    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    DNELs have not been derived for Alcohols, C18-22, distn. residues as no adverse effects were reported in any of the studies, even at the highest recommended doses (i.e., No hazard identified). The decision to not derive DNELs is supported by the latest 2012 ECHA guidance document on information requirements Part B: Hazard Assessment (B.8.4, p.56):

    If no adverse effects have been observed in studies at the highest recommended concentrations/doses tested, this would normally indicate that no hazard has been identified and no DNEL or PNEC can be derived and hence exposure assessment for that route of exposure, type of effect or protection target would not be needed

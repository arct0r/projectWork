    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    low hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    6 -HNA is exclusively imported as a reacted monomer in a polymer backbone. The risk of exposure of the general population to 6 -HNA, while not quantifiable, is considered to be negligible.Guidance provided by the German REACH helpdesk stated that "in the case of a monomer, its life-cycle ends with the polymerisation step. As this step takes place outside of the EU, there is no identified use for the monomer within the EU, as it is not imported as such." This approach was confirmed by the German REACH helpdesk of the German Federal Institute for Occupational Safety and Health.

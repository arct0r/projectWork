    Additional information - workers

    Diniobium pentaoxide is not bioavailable via any foreseeable route of exposure, nor have any significant local effects been observed via the dermal and inhalation routes. This entails a lack of toxicological hazards. Therefore, no toxicological threshold values have to be proposed for diniobium pentaoxide.

    Additional information - General Population

    There is no foreseeable exposure scenario for the general population for diniobium pentaoxide. Diniobium pentaoxide is not bioavailable via any foreseeable route of exposure, nor have any significant local effects been observed via the dermal and inhalation routes. This entails a lack of toxicological hazards. Therefore, no toxicological threshold values have to be proposed for the substance.

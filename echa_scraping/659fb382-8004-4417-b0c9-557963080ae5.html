    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    The oral NOAEL for developmental toxicity of 150 mg/kg was used to base the inhalation DNEL on. Route-to-route extrapolation is applied in accordance with the ECHA REACH Guidance on Information Requirements R.8 by dividing by the 8-hr respiratory volume of the rat (0.38 m3/kg bw), and corrected for light activity of the worker (6.7 m3/ 10 m3). The derived concentration is corrected for differences in oral and inhalation absorption with a default factor of 2, in accordance with the ECHA REACH Guidance, arriving at a NOAEC worker of 132.24 mg/m3.

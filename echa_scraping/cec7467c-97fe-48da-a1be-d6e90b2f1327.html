    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    medium hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    No systemic adverse effects have been identified for TaCl5. Due to hydrolysis and subsequent release of HCl, TaCl5 is classified corrosive to the skin (Category 1B/1C). Moreover, classification for H335 is warranted due to the generation of HCl, which is in line with the harmonised classification of hydrochloric acid.

    According to the ECHA Guidance on information requirements and chemical safety assessment Part E: Risk Characterisation (Version 2, Nov 2012), substances classified for Skin Corrosion Category 1B, Category 1C and H335 (respiratory irritation) are allocated to the moderate hazard band.

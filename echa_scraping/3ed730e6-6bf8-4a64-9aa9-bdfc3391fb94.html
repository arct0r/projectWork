    Explanation for hazard conclusion

    Additional information - workers

    3-chloro-1,2-propanediol is handled as transported intermediate used only under strictly controlled conditions. Therefore under normal conditions repeated exposure is only expected via the inhalative route on a very low level. only in case of accidents, there might be acute exposure to higher amounts of the substance via the skin or the inhalative route.

    Additional information - General Population

    3-chloro-1,2-propanediol is a transported intermediate used only under strictly controlled conditions and any consumer uses are "Adviced Against". Therefore, only in the case of accidents should the environment be exposed to 3-chloro-1,2-propanediol.

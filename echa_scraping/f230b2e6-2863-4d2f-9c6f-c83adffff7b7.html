    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    [Empty]
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    HBr is corrosive at relatively low airborne concentrations and forms hydrobromic acid in water dissociating to hydrogen and bromide ions. In consequence, oral and dermal DNELs for HBr exposure cannot be derived. HBr levels should not breach the IOELV value (2000/39/EC) for a 15-minute inhalation exposure.

    Additional information - General Population

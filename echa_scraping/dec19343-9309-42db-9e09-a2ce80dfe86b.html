    Additional information - workers

    The values listed above are derived for the hydrolysis product formaldehyde.

    For ethylene glycol the following value may be used:

    Worker DNEL systemic effects 1.5 mg/kg bw/d, DNEL local effects short-term 6.33, medium-term 2.75 and long-term 1.38 mg/m3

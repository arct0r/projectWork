    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    high hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The long-term and acute/short term inhalation DNELs for systemic effects as well as long and short term inhalation DNELs for local effects were not derived since no hazard was identified based on the low volatile properties of the substance (vapour pressure < 1x10E-5 kPa) and the substance handling under SCC conditions.

    The long-term dermal DNEL for systemic effects is derived on the basis that the substance is considered to be corrosive to skin category 1B (CLP). The substance therefore is considered to exhibit a medium hazard to workers (no threshold derived).

    The acute/short term dermal DNEL for systemic effects as well as the acute/short and long term dermal DNELs for local effects were not derived, since high hazard was identified based on physical chemical properties of the substance and on information provided by the production plant. The substance was considered to be corrosive.

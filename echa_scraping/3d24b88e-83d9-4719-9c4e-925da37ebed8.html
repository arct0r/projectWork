    Systemic effects

    Long term exposure

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    DNEL (Derived No Effect Level)
    Value
    0.063 mg/mÂ³
    Most sensitive endpoint
    repeated dose toxicity
    Route of original study
    By inhalation

    DNEL related information

    DNEL derivation method
    ECHA REACH Guidance
    Overall assessment factor (AF)
    10
    Dose descriptor starting point
    LOAEC
    Value
    0.63 mg/mÂ³
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    [Empty]
    Value
    mg/mÂ³
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    A LOAEL of 6.3 mg/day has been established based on side effects (hypoglycaemic episodes) after everyday treatment of diabetic persons with inhalation therapy of 6.3 mg human insulin /day. For workers inhaling 10 m3 air/working day this corresponds to a LOAEC of 6.3 mg/day / 10m3/day = 0.63 mg/m3

    DNEL = 0.63 mg/m3 / (10 (dose response) x 1 (interspecies) x1 (intraspecies) = 0.063 mg/m3

    AF for dose response relationship
    10
    Justification

    LOAEC to NOAEC extrapolation. Severe hypoglycaemic episodes have occurred, no indication of a specific NOAEC value.

    AF for differences in duration of exposure
    1
    Justification

    prolonged inhalation therapy

    AF for interspecies differences (allometric scaling)
    1
    Justification

    human data

    AF for other interspecies differences
    1
    Justification

    human data

    AF for intraspecies differences
    1
    Justification

    Diabetic persons are especially sensitive towards hypoglycaemiac episodes from insulin exposure, thus no further factor for workers is justified

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Conversion into an inhalatory rat NOAEC by dividing by 0.38 m3/kg (8-hour respiratory volume in the rat). Correction for activity driven differences in respiratory volume in workers compared to individuals at rest (6.7 m3/10 m3). Correction for exposure duration of animals vs. workers (7 days/5days).

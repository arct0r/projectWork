    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Route-to-route extrapolation (oral to inhalation) was performed according to the following conversion formula:

    NOAECcorr= NOAELoralx 1/0.38 [m3/kg/day] x ABSoral-rat/ABSinhal-humanx 6.7/10 [m3(8 h)] x fexpo-rat/fexpo-worker

    NOAECcorr= 15 x 1/0.38 x 100%/100% x 6.7/10 x 7/5 [mg/m3]

    NOAECcorr= 37.03 mg/m3

    Due to the corrosive nature of the substance, the inhalation absorption percentage is considered to be 100% as a worst-case approach.

    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Local effects

    Long term exposure

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    DNEL (Derived No Effect Level)
    Value
    13.3 mg/mÂ³
    Most sensitive endpoint
    repeated dose toxicity

    DNEL related information

    DNEL derivation method
    ECHA REACH Guidance
    Overall assessment factor (AF)
    7.5
    Dose descriptor
    LOAEC
    Value
    100 mg/mÂ³
    AF for dose response relationship
    3
    Justification

    A factor of 3 is considered sufficient for extrapolation from a LOAEC to a NOAEC (effects were only minimal and only in one animal in the low dose being the LOAEC)

    AF for differences in duration of exposure
    1
    Justification

    Not needed.

    AF for interspecies differences (allometric scaling)
    1
    Justification

    Assessment factor for allometric scaling is not needed here as the local effects (i.e. alkaline destruction of membranes) rather depend on local deposition than on metabolism.

    AF for other interspecies differences
    2.5
    Justification

    Humans would be more sensitive than animals to effects on the respiratory tract (Echa Guidance R8).

    AF for intraspecies differences
    1
    Justification

    No differences are assumed as the effects are solely local effects and the rat is a sufficient conservative model. Alkaline destruction of membranes is assumed to be the underlying reason here for the tissue degeneration. Moreover, a factor of 2.5 is already included since humans are considered more sensitive than animals to effects on the respiratory tract.

    AF for the quality of the whole database
    1
    Justification

    Actual guideline study and GLP-conform.

    AF for remaining uncertainties
    1
    Justification

    No remaining differences are assumed as the effects are solely local effects and the rat is a sufficient conservative model. Alkaline destruction of membranes is assumed to be the underlying reason here for the tissue degeneration.

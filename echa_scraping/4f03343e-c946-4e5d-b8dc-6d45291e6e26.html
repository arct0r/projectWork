    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The results of the acute inhalation/oral studies and repeated dose/reproductive screen study, did not show any biologically significant signs of toxicity and the substance is not classified in accordance with the CLP regulation. As such no hazard has been identified. There are no uses of the substance which can be linked with exposure to the general population and no consumer uses. As such exposure is expected to be minimal.

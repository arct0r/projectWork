    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    [Empty]
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The registration substance is a transported isolated intermediate and will therefore be used under strictly controlled conditions in line with article 18, 4.(b) of the REACH regulation EC1907/2006 which states "procedural and control technologies shall be used that minimise emission and any resulting exposure", and therefore no exposure is expected. A CSR is not needed for this substance and therefore DNEL values do not need to be calculated.

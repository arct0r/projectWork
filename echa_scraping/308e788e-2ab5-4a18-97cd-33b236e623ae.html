    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    low hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The effects seen in the chronic oral toxicity study in mice may be related to local irritation of the forestomach. In view of the severity of these effects (neoplasms), these effects were taken as starting point in a route to route extrapolation for dermal toxicity to derive a DNEL for dermal toxicity. In addition a safe concentration was assessed to prevent local effects to the skin based on data found in both human and animal studies.

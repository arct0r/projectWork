    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Human data are available due to the dual use of dapsone as a pharmaceutical and industrial chemical. The systemic DNEL protects also from effects of acute exposure. Long-term human health surveillance data are available from the medical literature and an epidemiological follow-up study performed >30 years after exposure with >20000 Australian Vietnam veterans, who have taken dapsone as malaria prophylaxis during their 1 year service. The dose of 25 mg/person/day is considered safe and without any adverse health effect.

    Workers - Hazard via dermal route

    Systemic effects

    Long term exposure

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    DNEL (Derived No Effect Level)
    Value
    46.8 mg/kg bw/day
    Most sensitive endpoint
    repeated dose toxicity
    Route of original study
    Oral

    DNEL related information

    DNEL derivation method
    ECHA REACH Guidance
    Overall assessment factor (AF)
    100
    Dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    4,683 mg/kg bw/day
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    4,683 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Route to route extrapolation was used as there is no dermal absorption information to estimate the systemic dose from the dermal dose, then the oral NOAEL was used as starting point. The dose descriptor starting point is obtained by conversion of the oral NOAEL to a dermal NOAEL considering a similar (very low) absorption by both oral and dermal route. Thereby, the corrected starting dermal NOAEL is equal to the oral NOAEL = 4683 mg/kg bw.

    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - General Population

    For general population no DNELs for acute toxicity were derived as the substance is not dangerous (acute oral toxicity oral and acute toxicity dermal > 2000 mg/kg (rats)).

    From the most sensitive endpoint NOAEL (250 mg/kg bw/day - effect on fertility - rats) the DNEL for long-term oral exposure and systemic effects was derived. Assesment factor 1200 was used for DNEL derivation. See ANNEX A to the connected Chemical safety report.

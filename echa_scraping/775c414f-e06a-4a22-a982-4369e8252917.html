    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    The dose descriptor of 600 mg/kg-day (NOAEL) from the 90-day oral gavage study was used. This oral NOAEL was converted (via route-to-route extrapolation) to a corrected inhalation NOAEC using ECHA Guidance and assuming ECHA default absorption values of 50% via the oral route of exposure and 100% via the inhalation route of exposure. The following Assessment Factors (AFs) were applied to the corrected dose descriptor of 529 mg/m3 to calculate the DNEL: Interspecies (remaining differences = 2.5); Intraspecies (5); Exposure duration (2); Dose-response (1); and Quality of database (1). Total AF applied = 25.

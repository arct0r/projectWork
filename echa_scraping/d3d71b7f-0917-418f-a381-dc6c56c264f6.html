    Additional information - workers

    In order to evaluate toxicological properties of the substance fatty acids, C9-13-neo-, barium salts, information on the assessment entities barium cation and neoacid category anions were considered. For a documentation and justification of that approach, please refer to the separate document attached to section 13, namely Read Across Assessment Report for fatty acids, C9-13-neo-, barium salts.

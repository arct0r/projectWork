    [Empty]

    [Empty]
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    50 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    It is assumed that, in general, dermal absorption will not be higher than oral absorption, so no default factor (i. e. factor 1) was introduced when performing the oral-to-dermal extrapolation (chapter R8.4.2). As a result, the modified dose descriptor starting point for dermal is equivalent to the NOAEL obtained from the 28 day OECD 422 oral study (50 mg/kg bw/d).

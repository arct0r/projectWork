    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Default (DNEL calculator), except for the dermal absorption = 3.54% instead of 100% based on results of an in vitro study performed in human cells.

    Therefore, the modified dose descriptor starting point is equal to 59.32 mg/kg bw/day  (ca. 1.5 * 7/5 * 100/3.54).

    Value
    117.35 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    The NOAEL was set at 42.6 mg/kg bw/day by oral route in rat during 90 days.

    Absorption oral versus dermal are calculated, taking into account the maximal dermal penetration rate of 36.3% observed in in vivo study OECDE 417, on rat.

    Oral absorption is assumed to be 100%

    NOAEL dermal = 42.6 mg/kg bw/d x (100/36.3) = 117.35 mg/kg bw/d

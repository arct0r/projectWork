    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    [Empty]
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Besides local effects revealed as irritation to the eyes, concluded only from an eye irritation in-vitro test (an eye irritation in-vivo test was not available), no local toxicity effects were observed in any of the animal studies carried out with GARDO TO 10451. Further, there was no evidence of local effects from occupational exposure. Thus, DNELs were based on systemic effects only.

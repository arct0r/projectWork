    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    Modification of starting point (NOAEL, rat = 2.5 mg/kg bw/d) : -Correction of respiratory volume for relevant duration: For 8h of exposure, the respiratory volume of rats and humans are 0.38 m3/kg bw and 6.7 m3/ person, respectively. The respiratory volume light activity for worker (8h exposure) is 10 m3/person. The correction factor is 1/0.38 x 6.7/10. -Correction for absorption difference between rat and human: No difference in inhalation and oral absorption are expected between rat and human. -Correction for absorption difference between inhalation and oral absorption: The oral absorption is estimated to 50%. According to ECHA Guidance â Chapter R8 (p25), the inhalation absorption is 100% : âif data on the starting route (oral) are available these should be used, but for the end route (inhalation), the worst case inhalation absorption should be assumed (i.e. factor 1)â. Therefore the correction factor is 0.5 (50/100). The corrected NOAEC is 2.2 mg/m3.

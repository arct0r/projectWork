    Additional information - workers

    Read-across proposed have been checked using the OECD Toolbox v. 1.1.01.

    Read-across with PG seems more relevant than with HPGM, as HPGM contains a structural alert (Ph-CH-CO-OCH3) for potential for skin sensitization as a result of protein conjugation by arylcarboxylate aminolysis (and PG and HPG, not). For other properties, both seem relevant.

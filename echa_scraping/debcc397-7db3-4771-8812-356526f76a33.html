    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Inhalation studies assessing the systemic toxicity of potassium 2-ethylhexanoate are not available. In the assessment of toxicity of potassium 2-ethylhexanoate, read-across to the assessment entities potassium and 2-ethylhexanoic acid is applied since the ions of potassium 2-ethylhexanoate determine its toxicity in biological compartments.

    Reliable data available for the assessment entities potassium and 2-ethylhexanoic acid indicate that the moiety of toxicological concern is the assessment entity 2 -ethylhexanoic acid.

    The toxicological hazard assessment is based on the most toxic moiety, i.e. 2 -ethylhexanoate anion, and the respective DNEL is recalculated for potassium 2-ethylhexanoate based on a maximum 2 -ethylhexanoic acid content of 84 %. Please refer to the section for the respective assessment entity.

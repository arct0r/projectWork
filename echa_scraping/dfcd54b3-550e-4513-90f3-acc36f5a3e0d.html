    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The substance is not acutely toxic via the oral or dermal routes,supporting information from exposure of humans reinforces the conclusions drawn from animal studies involving dermal exposure, the inhalation route is not considered to be of significance with respect to human exposure, and long-term studies are contraindicated for a substance that is used exclusively as a cosmetic ingredient in an industrial environment where risk mitigation measures are incorporated into operational procedures as a matter of routine. Furthermore, no evidence of skin irritation/corrosion, serious eye damage/irritation or skin sensitisation was found during performance of appropriate studies. Derivation of DNELs is therefore not possible in the absence of a suitable starting point for calculation.

    Additional information - General Population

    The substance is not acutely toxic via the oral or dermal routes, supporting information from exposure of humans reinforces the conclusions drawn from animal studies involving dermal exposure, the inhalation route is not considered to be of significance with respect to human exposure,and long-term studies are inappropriate because the neat substance is used only in the industrial environment and exposure of the general population will not take place. Furthermore, no evidence of skin irritation/corrosion, serious eye dame/irritation or skin sensitisation was found during performance of appropriate studies. Derivation of DNELs is therefore not possible in the absence of a suitable starting point for calculation.

    Starting point is a 90-day study in rat by oral route which establishing a NOAEL of 30 mg/kg bw/day for systemic toxicity. Corrected inhalation NOAEC = oral NOAELrat (30 mg/kg) * 1/0.38 * 6.7/10 = 52.9 mg/m3. No factor 2 route extrapolation from oral to inhalation. Due to low vapour pressure (0.18 Pa at 20 Â°C), relevant exposure is only possible as aerosol (no fine spraying, only larger droplets). Droplets will deposit mainly on upper airways, and will be subsequently swallowed following mucociliary transportation to pharynx. This results to no principal difference in absorption compared oral route.

    Additional information - General Population

    The uses indicated in this dossier are limited to industrial and professional users and exposure of the general population is not to be expected.

    Also assessment of indirect exposure is not needed as the tonnage is not above 1000 tpa, and the substance not is classified as CMR or toxic (R48 or STOT-RE).

    However, to allow for evaluation from indirect exposures via the environment, DNELs have been derived for systemic toxicity from long term exposures via all routes.

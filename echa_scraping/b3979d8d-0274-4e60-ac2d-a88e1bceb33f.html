    Additional information - workers

    Based on the very low bioaccessibility of the elements contained in this pigment, demonstrated by Knopf (2017), and the results of the available acute toxicity study for the oral and inhalation route does not indicate any reversible or irreversible adverse health effects, the derivation of effect levels for the substance is neither feasible nor required.

    General Population - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - General Population

    Based on the very low bioaccessibility of the elements contained in the pigment, demonstrated by Knopf (2017), and the assumption that the general population is only exposed to the pigment via consumer products no effect levels for the general population for the substance are derived. Furthermore the results of available acute toxicity study for the oral and inhalation route did not indicate any reversible or irreversible adverse health effects and thus, the derivation of effect levels for the substance is neither feasible nor required.

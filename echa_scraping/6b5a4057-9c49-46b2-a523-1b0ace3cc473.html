    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    The repeated oral and inhalation studies suggest similar effects, such as those on the kidneys, irrespective of route of exposure. If the respiratory rate (8 hour) of a worker is assumed to be 10 m3/person with a body weight of 70 kg the corrected NOAEL(dermal) is therefore: = 85 mg/m3 * 10 m3/70 kg = 12 mg/kg bw/day (8 hour day)

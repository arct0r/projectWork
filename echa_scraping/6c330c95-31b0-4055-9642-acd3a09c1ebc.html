    Dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    600 mg/kg bw/day
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    600 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    No specific information on dermal absorption taken into account.

    This approach starts from the assumption that dermal absorption will not be higher than oral absorption.

    For the substance this can be considered a conservative approach, as the supporting dermal absorption study by Meyer indicates that the derrmal uptake of the substance is very low. However, as the Meyer study is rated as Klimisch 4 due to its unusual test setup and limited documentation, and the study does not provide a quantitative outcome, the default assessment factor of 1 for oral to dermal route-to-route extrapolation is taken into account.

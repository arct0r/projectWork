    Additional information - workers

    There is an extensive database for CHDM.  The existing DNEL was based on 90-day rat studies, and there was also a rat developmental toxicity study available.  The initial DNEL was based on systemic toxicity from the rat 90-day study.

    The 2019 update to the CHDM dossier takes into account the addition of an extended 1-generation reproductive study. In that study, there was no affect on systemic or developmental toxicity at the highest dose of 700 mg/kg/day.

    As the initial DNEL was based on the NOAEL of 479 mg/kg/day, that value will continue to be used for the starting point of the DNEL.  One adjustment that will be made to the previous calculation will be the removal of the adjustment factor of 3 for the adequacy of the database with the addition of the extended 1-generation study.

    There is a slight, but non-significant decrease in the DNEL values as the previous version used ECETOC values which are no longer supported by ECHA.

     

    DNEL Derivation

     

    Inhalation DNEL:  The long-term systemic DNEL was calculated by taking the rat NOAEL from the 90 day study of 479 mg/kg/day and adjusting it to an inhalation dose by dividing by 0.38 m3/kg-BW. Oral bioavailability is corrected to 90% based on the available TK data. Workers are corrected for light work by correcting respiration from 6.7 m3/day to 10 m3/day. The correction values used are:

                   Intraspecies:                                    5 for worker, 10 for general population

                   Remaining interspecies:               2.5

                   Subchronic to chronic study:      2

                   

                   Calculations:

                                   Worker:  NOAEL 479 mg/kg * 1kg/0.38 m3BW (8 hr) = 1260.52 m3/kg-day.

                                   Correct for 90% bioavailability = 1260.52 m3/kg-day *0.9 = 1134.46 m3/kg-day

                                   Correct for light work = 1134.46 m3/kg-day * 0.67 = 760.08 m3/kg-day

                                   Apply total correction factors of 25 = 760.08 m3/kg-day /25 =30.4 m3/kg-day

     

    General population:  NOAEL 479 mg/kg * 1kg/1.15 m3/kg BW (24 hr) = 416.52 m3/kg-day.

                                   Correct for 90% bioavailability = 416.52 m3/kg-day *0.9 = 374.86 m3/kg-day

                                   Apply total correction factors of 50 = 374.86 m3/kg-day /50 =7.5 m3/kg-day

     

     

    Oral/Dermal DNEL: The long-term systemic DNEL was calculated by taking the rat NOAEL from the 90 day study of 479 mg/kg/day and adjusting it for oral bioavailability by correcting to 90% based on the available TK data. Workers are corrected for light work by correcting respiration from 6.7 m3/day to 10 m3/day. The correction values used are:

                   Intraspecies:                                    5 for worker, 10 for general population

                   Remaining interspecies:               2.5

                   Subchronic to chronic study:      2

                   Allometric scaling:                           4

                   

                   Calculations:

                                   Worker:  NOAEL 479 mg/kg

                                   Correct for 90% bioavailability = 479 mg/kg-day *0.9 = 431.1 mg/kg-day

                                   Correct for light work = 431.1 mg/kg-day * 0.67 = 288.83 mg/kg-day

                                   Apply total correction factors of 100 = 760.08 m3/kg-day /100 =2.88 mg/kg-day

     

                                   General Population: NOAEL 479 mg/kg

                                   Correct for 90% bioavailability = 479 mg/kg-day *0.9 = 431.1 mg/kg-day

                                   Apply total correction factors of 200 = 760.08 m3/kg-day /200 =1.44 mg/kg-day

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    No route to route extrapolation necessary, as route in original study is via inhalation.

    Additional information - workers

    In the absence of classification for systemic and local effects after acute inhalation exposure and local effects after dermal exposure to 1,3 -Dioxolane for acute toxicity, no acute DNEL's are considered applicable.

    In the absence of local effects after repeated exposure to 1,3 -Dioxolane, no long-term DNELs for local effects are considered applicable.

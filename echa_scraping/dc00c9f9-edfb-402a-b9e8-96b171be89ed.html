    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion

    Additional information - workers

    The test substance is not irritating for the eye and therefore classification or labeling is not warranted in accordance with EU Classification, Labeling and Packaging of Substances and Mixtures (CLP) Regulation No. 1272/2008. Based on this information, calculation of DNEL for local effects for the eye is not necessary.

    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    An oral absorption rate of 5% is selected for Fatty acids, tall-oil, manganese salts, as established in the bioelution studies in gastric and interstitial medium. 

    As the manganese cation is the constituent driving the toxicity of Fatty acids, tall-oil, manganese salts, a default dermal absorption ratio of 1% for the manganese cation is selected for the substance Fatty acids, tall-oil, manganese salts. This default dermal absorption ratio of 1% for the manganese cation is based on the HERAG FACT SHEET Assessment of occupational dermal exposure and dermal absorption for metals and inorganic metal compounds.

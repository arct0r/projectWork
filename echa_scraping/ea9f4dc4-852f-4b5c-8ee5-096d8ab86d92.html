    Additional information - workers

    No DNEL was derived since no adverse effects were observed in the available toxicity studies performed with the target substance and/or structural analogues of the target substance glycerol monomyristate (CAS 27214 -38 -6). Therefore, the registration substance does not meet the criteria for any of the hazard classes, categories or properties set out in Article 14(4) of Regulation (EC) No 1907/2006 and Annex I of Regulation (EC) No 1272/2008.

    Additional information - General Population

    Ethylenediamine is not used in applications for the general public/consumers in the EU; it is mainly used as intermediate, thus consumed in the production processes. As such no DNELs are derived for the general population except for the long-term inhalation and oral DNEL (systemic effects) to be used in the assessment of exposure of man via the environment.

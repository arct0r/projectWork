    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Ditantalum pentoxide is not acutely toxic after oral administration. The oral LDâ value is as high as > 8000 mg/kg. Ditantalum pentoxide did not reveal skin or eye corrosion/irritating properties when tested in rabbits, nor did it produce any sensitisation when tested in a guinea pig maximisation study. Therefore deriving DNELs for systemic effects are not required. Based on results of in vitro studies, ditantalum pentoxide is considered not to possess any genotoxic potential.

    In a repeat-dose combined reproductive/developmental toxicity study (OECD 422) performed in rats on a more soluble tantalum compound (tantalum pentachloride) no adverse effects were reported.

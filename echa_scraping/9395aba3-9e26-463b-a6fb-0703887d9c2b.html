    Additional information - workers

    In order to evaluate toxicological properties of the substance Fatty acids, C6-19-branched, zinc salts, information on the assessment entities zinc cation and fatty acids, C6-19-branched anions were considered. For a documentation and justification of that approach, please refer to the separate document attached to section 13, namely Read Across Assessment Report for Fatty acids, C6-19-branched, zinc salts.

    A background document on the derivation of the DNEL values is also attached as a separate report in section 13 of the IUCLID.

    Hazard assessment conclusion

    Explanation for hazard conclusion

    Additional information - workers

    The DNELs are derived from the available test data for the substance, with assessment factors applied to offer an additional layer of protection (as a means of accounting for any uncertainty). The DNELs are derived from a developmental toxicity study that is available on the substance itself. While there are data available from sub-chronic toxicity studies on the substance itself, other DNELs were derived from the more conservative findings of a sub-acute inhalation study with a larger assessment factor applied than would have been the case with the sun-chronic studies. Overall, the base data that the DNELs have been derived from produces the most conservative DNELs.

    Additional information - workers

    3 -chloropropene is handled as transported intermediate only, under strictly controlled conditions. Therefore under normal conditions repeated exposure is only expected via the inhalative route on a very low level. Only in cases of accidents, there might be acute exposure to higher amounts of the substance via the skin or the inhalative route.

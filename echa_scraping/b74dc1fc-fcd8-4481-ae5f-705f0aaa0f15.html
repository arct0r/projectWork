    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    high hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Chlorotrimethylsilane hydrolyses very rapidly in moist air and in contact with tissues to form hydrogen chloride (HCl) and trimethylsilanol (TMS). Local effects (corrosion) are therefore influenced by the formation of HCl.

    Additional information - General Population

    There are no consumer uses of chlorotrimethylsilane, and no potential for exposure to humans via the environment. It is therefore not necessary to calculate DNEL values for the general population.

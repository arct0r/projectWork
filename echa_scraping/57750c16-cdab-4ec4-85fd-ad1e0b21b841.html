    Additional information - workers

    2 -methylbutene is considered to be an in vivo mutagen (Muta cat 2, H341 under CLP) based upon data included in the dossier. A DNEL has been derived from the most sensitive endpoint (repeated-dose toxicity), using the ECHA REACH Guidance and the DNEL calculator. Systemic DNELs for inhalation and dermal endpoints were derived.

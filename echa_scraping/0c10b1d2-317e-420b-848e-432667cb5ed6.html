    Additional information - workers

    The chronic health effects for this substance are based on those of its analogue: acrylamide. The long-term dermal and inhalation DMELs were derived from the DMEL obtained from chronic oral exposure in a 2-year drinking-water study in rats (Friedman et al., 1995). The BMDL10 derived from this study is 0.45 mg/kg/day. The dermal and inhalation absorption factors applied were obtained from a study on humans for dermal absorption (Fennell et al., 2001) and in rats for inhalation absorption (Sumner et al, 2001).

    Starting point: NOAEL for developmental toxicity of 40 mg/kg bw/d

    Modification of starting point involves conversion of rat oral exposure to human inhalation exposure using ECHA Ch R8, Fig. 8.3. Human NOAEC = Oral NOAEL X 1/0.38 mÂ³/kg/d X 0.5 absn. Default X 6.7/10 = 35.3 mg/mÂ³

    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    No adverse effects were observed in the oral 28 day repeated dose / reproduction / developmental toxicity study and in the 13 -week feeding study in rats up to the highest dose tested. It is therefore concluded that the substance is not hazardous to human health.

    Furthermore, the substance is neither classified for acute oral toxicity and acute dermal toxicity nor for eye/skin irritation and sensitization.

    No risk assessment is considered necessary for worker's exposure to the substance.

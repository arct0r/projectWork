    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    An inhalation DNEL was derived with a route-to route extrapolation, based on the chosen oral NOAEL from other salts.

    A LOAEL of 145 mg/kg/day was chosen from the 9 month study

    With AF 25, this equates to 5.8 mg/kg/day or 406 mg/day for 70 kg worker; with inhalation rate 10 m3/shift = 40.6 mm/m3

    Limit set at 10 mg/m3 for safe working practice

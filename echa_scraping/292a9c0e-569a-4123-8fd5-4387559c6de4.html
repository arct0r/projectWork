    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    [Empty]
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Since cobalt aluminium oxide is only imported as already included into the matrix and the matrix particles are greater than 100 m, a risk potential for the worker is not expected and DMELs do not need to be derived. Furthermore, cobalt aluminium oxide is only used as an intermediate under strictly controlled conditions.

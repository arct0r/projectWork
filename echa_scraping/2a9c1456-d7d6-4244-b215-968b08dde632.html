    Additional information - workers

    Regarding acute and long-term local effects, local irritation on skin and/or mucous membranes, e. g. eye and respiratory tract as primary effect can be expected for fatty acids with a chain length â¤C12 due to the corrosive/irritation properties of the short- and mid-chain fatty acids C6 â C12, respectively.

    Since no adequate dose descriptor for the corrosion/irritation effects are observed, no DNEL is calculated for local effects. However, appropriate risk management measures will be identified.

    According to table E.3-1 in the âGuidance on information requirements and chemical safety assessment Part E: Risk Characterisation (ECHA, 2016), fatty acids, C8-18 (CAS# 90990-08-2) is allocated to the moderate hazard category based on classification as Skin Corr. 1C (H314) according to Regulation (EC) No 1272/2008.

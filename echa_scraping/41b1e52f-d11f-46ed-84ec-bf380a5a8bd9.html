    Additional information - workers

    For all details see CSR section 5.11

    DNEL-inhalation

    DNELacute

    A Worker-DNEL-acutefor the inhalatory route has not been derived since diformal is classified for acute inhalation toxicity. The critical DNEL is 6.25 mg/m3.

     

    DNELlongterm

    A Worker-DNEL-longtermfor the inhalatory route has been derived. The critical DNEL is 1.76 mg/m3.

     

    DNEL-dermal

    DNELlongterm

    A Worker-DNEL-longtermfor the dermal route been derived. The critical DNEL is 1.25 mg/kg bw/day.

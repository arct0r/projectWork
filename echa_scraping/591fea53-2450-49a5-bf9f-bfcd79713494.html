    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point

    Due to lack of route specific information for

    inhalation, further extrapolation may be made from

    the oral NOAEL. A default factor of 2 has been

    applied assuming that half of the applied dose would

    be absorbed via the oral route compared to the

    inhalation route according to the current guidance

    document (R8, ECHA 2008). A conservative NOAEC

    for the general population may be derived according

    to the procedure recommended in the guidance

    document (R8, ECHA 2008). NOAEC corrected

    inhalation (general population) =

    40*(1/1.15)*(50/100) = 17mg/mg3. An assessment

    factor (AF) of 50 may be used. Interspecies:

    allometric scaling = 1; remaining differences = 2.5.

    Intraspecies = 10. Exposure duration = 2. Doseresponse

    = 1. Quality of data = 1.

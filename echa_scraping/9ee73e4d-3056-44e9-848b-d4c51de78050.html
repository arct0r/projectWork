    DNEL related information

    Overall assessment factor (AF)
    50
    Dose descriptor starting point
    [Empty]
    Value
    [Empty]
    Modified dose descriptor starting point
    NOAEL
    Value
    20 mg/kg bw/day
    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    SCCS (2009) concluded a chronic NOAEL of 10 mg/kg bw/d of cetrimonium bromide dosed through drinking water. As the dermal absorption rate in rats (approx 3%) is considered less than the oral absorption rate in rats (approx. 6%), this oral NOAEL value may be extrapolated to a dermal NOAEL value of 20 mg/kg bw/d in rats.

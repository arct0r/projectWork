    Explanation for the modification of the dose descriptor starting point 
    Dose descriptor: NOAEL (oral) = 300 mg/kg/d. NOAEC corrected = 300 x 1/0.38 x 6.7/10 x 0,5 x 7/5 = 370,3 mg/m3

    1

    Justification

    1
    Justification

    The key study chosen for DNEL calculation is considered as a reliable study.

    AF for remaining uncertainties
    1
    Justification

    No additional factor is added for read-across because the worst case scenario is planned (the study was performed on a C4 methacrylate and the registered substance is a C10 methacrylate, less absorbed by definition than C4).

    Additional information - workers

    The assessment factors were selected based on the guidance document section R.8-4.3.1, AFs realting to the extrapolation procedure.

    No DNELS for acute systemic or local effects were derived. Long-term DNEL in combination with suitable safety measures for porcessing/packing with regards to classification as eye irritant are considered sufficient to ensure that exposure will not occur at the work place.

    Additional information - workers

    Due to the corrosiveness, the deviation of DNEL values is inappropriate. Only the deviation of an oral DNEL, due to the neutralization of sodium hydroxide by gastric acid with the formation of sodium chloride and epsilon-Caprolactam may be justified (with restrictions).

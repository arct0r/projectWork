    Workers - Hazard for the eyes

    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    no hazard identified
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    The substance is not classified for human health.

    Acute toxicity studies by the oral and dermal routes showed no evidence of systemic toxicity. The available repeated dose oral toxicity studies show no evidence of systemic toxicity (no adverse effects seen at the limit doses).

    No DNELs for systemic effects are considered necessary.

    The substance is not a skin irritant, skin sensitiser or eye irritant. No DNELs or qualitative hazard assessment for local effects are considered necessary.

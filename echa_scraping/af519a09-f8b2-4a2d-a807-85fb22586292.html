    Additional information - workers

    Assessment factors (AF) for derivation of DNELs:

    Workers/short-term exposure, systemic and local effects, inhalative route: Calculation according to TRGS 900 (Germany). Workers/long-term exposure, systemic and local effects, inhalative route: Calculation according to TRGS 900 (Germany).

    The following DN(M) EL could not be derived:

    -Dermal DN(M) EL for acute/short term effects

    -local and systemic effects

    -Dermal DN(M) EL for long-term exposure

    -local and systemic effects

    Because an exposure of general population is not relevant, DN(M)ELs for the general population are not required

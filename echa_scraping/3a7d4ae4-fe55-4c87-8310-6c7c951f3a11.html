    Local effects

    Hazard assessment conclusion
    high hazard (no threshold derived)
    Explanation for hazard conclusion
    [Not publishable]

    Additional information - workers

    Chlorodimethylsilane is a volatile liquid which hydrolyses very rapidly in moist air and in contact with tissues to form hydrogen chloride (HCl) and dimethylsilanol.

    Hydrogen chloride (HCl)

    An EU long-term inhalation Occupational Exposure Limit (OEL) has been set for HCl as 8 mg/m3(8 h TWA) in Commission Directive 2000/39/EC.
